A powerful, real-time interactive equation visualizer. Plot any mathematical expression as a function of x, explore graphs with pan & zoom, use dynamic variable sliders, and export your work as PNG or SVG.

✦ Real-time expression validation
✦ Dynamic sliders for variables (a, b, …)
✦ Pan, zoom & viewport controls
✦ Math keyboard for easy input
✦ Export as PNG or SVG
✦ Save & load equations locally
